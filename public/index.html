Welcome to Ice Breakr !

Unsure of how to get your team acquainted at your first meeting? With Ice Breakr, you and your team can find the perfect way to "break the ice" even in a virtual setting. Ice Breakr is a brand new web service that is perfect for team meetings, classroom settings, or for any group seeking a fun and easy way to get to know one another.

How to Get Started: Hosting a Room

Are you looking to introducte the members of your group to one another?
Here's how to create a room for your group to join.

  1. 1. Select "Create a Room" at the top of the site.
  2. 2. Enter your name. ( This name will be displayed as the room's host when participants join. )
  3. 3. A "room code" will be generated. Participants will use this to join your room.
  4. 4. Confirm that all information is correct, then enter the room.
  5. 5. Share your screen with your meeting participants. ( This will work online, via a screensharing service, or in person, as long as everyone present can see your screen. )
  6. 6. Instruct your participants to follow the "How to Get Started: Joining a Room" instruction set on our website below.
  7. 7. When all participants have loaded into the room, you, as the host, will be able to choose between several Ice Breakr games.
  8. 8. Load games for your participants, and have fun!
How to Get Started: Joining a Room

Has your meeting introduced Ice Breakr for group introduction?
Here's how to join a room created by your meeting host.

  1. 1. Select "Join a Room" at the top of the site.
  2. 2. Enter your name. ( The host will be able to identify your responses by your name. )
  3. 3. Enter the room code provided by your host.
  4. 4. Confirm that all information is correct, then enter the room.
  5. 5. Wait for the host to select an Ice Breakr game. You will see a prompt on your screen when a new game is selected.
  6. 6. Follow game instructions as they appear or as they are explained, and have fun!
